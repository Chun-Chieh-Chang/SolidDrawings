# Modeling Script Guide: SolidWorks Exercise 11 (Video: -LL3eSTyWe8)

## Feature Decomposition

### 1. Base Feature (Extrude)
- **Sketch Plane**: Top Plane
- **Geometry**: Circle
- **Constraints**: 
    - Center coincident with Origin.
    - Diameter = 71mm.
- **Feature**: Extrude Boss/Base
    - Depth: 9mm

### 2. Center Keyway Cut (Cut-Extrude)
- **Sketch Plane**: Top Face of Base
- **Geometry**: Circle (D=47.5mm) and Parallel Lines (15mm gap)
- **Constraints**:
    - Circle Center coincident with Origin.
    - Lines symmetric to the center axis, gap = 15mm.
    - Cut profile: The intersection of the lines and circle (trimmed).
- **Feature**: Extrude Cut
    - Depth: 20.5mm from bottom (or Through All if cutting through the 9mm base).

### 3. Corner Fillets
- **Feature**: Fillet
    - Radius: 4mm
    - Target: The 4 vertical edges generated by the center keyway cut.

### 4. Flange Holes (Cut-Extrude & Pattern)
- **Sketch Plane**: Top Face of Base
- **Geometry**: Circle
- **Constraints**:
    - Located on a Pitch Circle (Construction Circle) of Radius 59mm.
    - Diameter = 5.5mm.
- **Feature**: Extrude Cut (Through All)
- **Feature**: Circular Pattern
    - Axis: Center Origin / Face edge.
    - Count: 4 or 6 (assuming 4 evenly spaced for simulation purposes).

## Constraint Implementation Focus
- **Concentric**: Ensuring holes and base share the origin.
- **Symmetric/Distance**: Maintaining the 15mm gap for the center cut.
- **Circular Pattern**: Generating replicated features parametrically.
