# 3D-Builder v1.5 Release Delivery Report

> **Delivery Date**: 2026-05-24

---

## 1. Executive Summary

3D-Builder v1.5 marks a strategic leap from a single-part modeler to a comprehensive multi-component design system. This release introduces industrial-grade assembly constraints, professional sectioning capabilities, and a density-driven physical analysis engine.

---

## 2. Key Achievements

### 2.1 3D Assembly Mate Solver (P5-1)
- **Constraint Engine**: Extended the Scipy NR solver to 6-DOF rigid body optimization.
- **Mate Support**: Fully functional Coincident, Concentric, and Distance mates between persistent topological references.
- **Async Execution**: Moved solving logic to the heavy backend to ensure UI responsiveness during complex assembly updates.

### 2.2 Section View & Advanced Drafting (P5-2)
- **B-Rep Slicing**: Integrated BRepAlgoAPI_Section for real-time model clipping.
- **HLR Integration**: Automated hidden-line removal for sectioned views, enabling clear internal visualization for complex parts.

### 2.3 Material & Physical Analysis (P5-3)
- **Material Library**: Introduced standardized densities for Steel, Aluminum, and common Plastics.
- **Physical Fidelity**: Replaced geometric volume reports with accurate Mass (grams) and Center of Mass calculations based on material properties.
